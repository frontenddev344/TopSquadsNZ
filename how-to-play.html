How To Play

The goal of this guide is to maximise the potential of the TopSquadsNZ app for Kiwi sports lovers. Regardless of your level of experience, this guide will provide you with the knowledge you need to use the software, create strong teams, and take advantage of the thrills of fantasy sports.

Getting Starter

Install and get TopSquadsNZ:

  • On your mobile device, go to Google Play or the App Store.
  • Download the TopSquadsNZ Fantasy Sports app by searching for it.
  • To create your account, install the app and run it.

Setup a TopSquadsNZ account:

  • Use your social accounts or email address to sign up.
  • Select a safe password and a distinctive username.
  • Please use the supplied verification process to confirm your account.

Browse the TopSquadsNZ app:

  • Learn how to use the user-friendly interface of the app.
  • Look through sections like player stats, teams, and leagues.

Creating your fantasy squad:

  • Join a League on TopSquadsNZ
  • Either look through and join existing leagues, or start your own private league.
  • To compete with the top competitors, think about taking part in open championships.

Creating a TopSquadsNZ Team:

  • Use the automatic draft tool or take part in a live draft.
  • Choose players according to real performance data.
  • Take note of player prices and position requirements.

Tips for TopSquadsNZ Player Selection:

  • Keep up with recent appearances, suspensions, and injuries to the players.
  • Increase team diversity to reduce the damage of teams losing.
  • Make strategic decisions by utilising expert analysis and player opinions.

Manage your TopSquadsNZ team:

  • Make lineup adjustments before every game week.
  • Take into account the player's form, upcoming games, and any injuries.

Make transfers and trades:

  • To make your TopSquadsNZ squad stronger, trade players with other managers.
  • Transfer players strategically in accordance with their availability and output.

Keep yourself updated with TopSquadsNZ:

  • Keep up with the latest sports news and updates.
  • Keep tabs on team chemistry, injuries, and suspensions.

How to win with TopSquadsNZ:

  • Learn how to use the TopSquadsNZ scoring system.
  • Get points for a variety of in-game accomplishments, such goals, assists, clean sheets, and more.

Climb the Leaderboard:

  • Compete in the TopSquadsNZ Leagues against other coaches.
  • Win points to climb up the leaderboard and win rewards.

Celebrate your victories:

  • Take pleasure in the accomplishment of creating a winning fantasy team.
  • Interact with the TopSquadsNZ community, talk about tactics, and acknowledge accomplishments.

Thank you for joining TopSquadsNZ as a fantasy sports manager! To improve your TopSquadsNZ Fantasy Sports experience, refer to these instructions. Remember to have fun with fantasy sports and the friendship of Kiwi sports enthusiasts, whether you want fun or competition in our leagues. I hope the TopSquadsNZ brings you success and joy! Good luck!
